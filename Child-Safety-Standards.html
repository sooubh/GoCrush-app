GoCrush Child Safety Standards

GoCrush is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and secure dating environment. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, trafficking, or any activity that harms minors.

Important: GoCrush is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. Minors are not permitted to create accounts or use the platform.

1. Age Restriction Policy

GoCrush is a dating platform designed only for adults aged 18 years and above. Any account found to belong to a minor will be suspended or permanently removed.

  • Minimum age requirement: 18 years.
  • Accounts suspected of being underage may be reviewed.
  • Users may be required to verify their age when necessary.

2. Prohibited Content

The following content and activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).
  • Sexualization of minors.
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors.
  • Human trafficking involving minors.
  • Sharing, requesting, promoting, or distributing illegal material involving children.
  • Any activity violating child protection laws.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

GoCrush provides in-app reporting tools that allow users to report:

  • User profiles.
  • Messages and conversations.
  • Photos and uploaded content.
  • Harassment, abuse, or suspicious behavior.
  • Potential child safety concerns.

Reports are reviewed as quickly as possible by our moderation process.

4. Enforcement Actions

Violations of these standards may result in:

  • Immediate content removal.
  • Permanent account suspension.
  • Device-level restrictions where applicable.
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities when legally required.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

GoCrush cooperates with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations when required by applicable laws and regulations.

Content involving child exploitation may be preserved and reported to relevant authorities as required by law.

6. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining a safe environment and must:

  • Follow all applicable laws.
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately.
  • Avoid sharing inappropriate or illegal content.
  • Respect platform safety guidelines.
