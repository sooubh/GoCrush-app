Account Deletion Policy

At Gocrush, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your data. You can delete your account at any time.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all your matches, messages, photos, and profile information will be wiped from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Gocrush app.
  2. Tap on the Profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Go to Settings (the gear icon).
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision in the popup dialog.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you trigger the "Delete Account" process, the following actions occur immediately:

  • Authentication Account: Your login credentials (Email/Google/Phone) are permanently removed from our Authentication system.
  • Profile Data: Your name, age, bio, interests, and preferences are deleted from our Firestore database.
  • Media: All photos and verification selfies stored in our Storage buckets are permanently deleted.
  • Interactions: All likes, superlikes, and swipes you have sent or received are removed.
  • Messages: Your chat history with all matches is wiped from our Real-Time Database.
  • Matches: Active matches are terminated, and your profile will no longer appear in the match lists of other users.
